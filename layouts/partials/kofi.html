FAQs

What if I've never made books before or not very much?
Not a problem at all! I make step by step tutorials, plus you only need to reach out to me on the platform for help.

What if I can't draw?
No worries - first I'll show you, and with practice you'll be able to draw what you want, and second however you represent things in your illustrations is legit and celebrated.

Can I cancel at anytime?
Absolutely! There is no contract, just a recurring monthly subscription payment, so you can cancel any time.
